Features Designed For Early Learners

Toddler Phonics strips away the noise and focus-stealers, leaving a beautifully clean, highly educational interface for toddlers.

🔤

Interactive Guessing Game

Encourage your child's natural curiosity. Hide letters or images behind interactive cards that flip to reveal adorable illustrations like animals and objects.

🔊

Clear Audio Voiceovers

Every letter name, word, and phonics sound has clear voice pronunciations built-in. Just tap to listen, repeat, and master pronunciation.

🛠️

Parent Customizations

Tailor the learning deck to your child. Omit specific letters they have already mastered, or randomize the sequence to test their active recall.

🌙

Gentle Dark Mode

Perfect for winding down in the evening. Keep screens soft on sensitive eyes with a beautiful dark interface.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Your privacy and your child's safety are our highest priorities. This privacy policy describes how the Toddler Phonics application handles data.

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information, location data, or device identifiers. Toddler Phonics is fully self-contained and does not require you to create an account, register, or provide any email addresses or other private details.

2. No Analytics and Offline Usage

The app does not track usage patterns, statistics, or metrics. All customizations (such as dark mode preference, word list selection, and omitted letters list) are stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage and are never transmitted to any servers.

3. No Ads or In-App Purchases

Toddler Phonics is entirely ad-free and contains no third-party software development kits (SDKs) or tracking scripts. There are no hidden in-app purchases or subscription options.

4. Kid-Friendly Compliance

Our app is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and international guidelines for children's apps. We collect zero data from children.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at support@matthuberty.com.
